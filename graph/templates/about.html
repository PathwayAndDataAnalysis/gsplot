Network Biology Labratory

The Network Biology Laboratory at the University of Massachusetts Boston focuses on modeling and analyzing biological systems at the molecular level. We develop methods and software tools to:

  • Increase our understanding in biological molecular mechanisms.
  • Make causal bridges between existing knowledge and new experiment results.
  • Efficient visualization of known and hypothesized relationships between biological molecules.
  • And more!
